Decentralized and Secure Digital Operational Market Using Block-Chain

The mercurial ecosystem of the fin-tech environment has the potential of being truly revolutionized by the Blockchain technology. The game-changing innovation that has come into the big picture because of Blockchain is due to its decentralized and distributed infrastructure which rules out the middleman and builds unshaken trust between the business and consumers.

Most small and mid-sized businesses operate within slim margins and any minute delay in payment can result in heavy losses and threaten the very existence of their businesses. Buyers are forced to release funds without any prior guarantee on delivery of products and sellers have to put their blind faith to test in order to receive their rightful remittance. Furthermore, safety of the software-based encryption wallet is also at stake since its security cannot be guaranteed. This paper discusses the prospective use of autonomous and decentralized storage of Inter Planetary File System (IPFS) to store and share design files and additional product specifications. This kind of peer-to-peer storage of data demonstrates the working principle of our proposed solution for secure business transactions.

In addition, we discuss comprehensive security and cost analysis of the proposed solution underlining its efficiency with respect to salient safety and performance requirements. Furthermore, we design a secure Blockchain-based lightweight wallet to protect simplified payment verification (SPV). In order to defend the validation procedure of the transaction, we bring forward a Secure Mutual Authentication Protocol (SMAP) based on the Universal 2nd Factor (U2F) protocol for mobile payment. To assure genuine service, we use the SHA-256 algorithm and an asymmetric cryptosystem for achieving mutual authentication that can resist unwanted intrusion and cyber attacks. This encapsulates the account information of the associated parties throughout the mobile-transaction process.

The exposition put forward utilizes smart contracts to govern B2B payments and trace financial transactions involving the candidates belonging to the business model. The ability of our proposed Blockchain-based solution to trace the different manufacturing stages of the product and promote trust in B2B payments among the participating stakeholders through different smart tools allows buyers and sellers to break free from the decades-old financial infrastructure and engage in straightforward business.
